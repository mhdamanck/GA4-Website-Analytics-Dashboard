Website Traffic Analysis | Internship Project

Tools Used: Google Analytics 4, Excel, Power BI

Project Overview

As part of my internship at Wizzycom AbacusMaster, I analyzed website traffic and user behavior for the international market, focusing on the United States audience.

The objective was to identify audience trends, engagement patterns, traffic sources, and landing page performance to support marketing strategy and campaign planning.

Key Insights
74% of users were new visitors.
Homepage showed higher bounce rates than intent-based landing pages.
California, Texas, and Florida emerged as high-potential regions.
Female users represented the majority of engaged visitors.
Mobile social traffic showed lower engagement, highlighting optimization opportunities.
Business Impact

The analysis provided actionable insights for campaign planning, audience targeting, and website optimization initiatives.
